Mr. Rajat Kumar Goyal

I am Rajat, and I am currently pursuing a Master’s (MTech.) in Quantum Technologies in IDRP at the Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur. Before joining here, I completed a master’s (MSc.) in Physics at the Department of Physics and Astronomy at the National Institute of Technology Rourkela. My Research interests include Quantum Materials Devices and Sensors, Semiconductors Technology, Various types of materials and their applications (Batteries, photodetectors, etc.), and Utilization of Machine Learning and Computational Tools in Experimental Physics. Basically, I want to work on a bridge between Physics and Technology through a multidisciplinary approach.
